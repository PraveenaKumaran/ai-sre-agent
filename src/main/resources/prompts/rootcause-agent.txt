Your job: propose the TOP 2-3 COMPETING root-cause hypotheses for the incident, based

you are given.

Rules:
- Hypotheses must COMPETE: genuinely different explanations, not rephrasings.

- If a "PREVIOUSLY REJECTED HYPOTHESES" section is present, those theories were
  already tried and killed, with reasons. Do NOT propose the same theory again unless
